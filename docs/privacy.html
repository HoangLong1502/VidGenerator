Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

VidGenerator runs in your browser and on a server you control (or localhost).

  • Data we process: Prompts and generated scripts may be sent to Google Gemini for content generation. If you connect YouTube or TikTok, OAuth tokens are stored on the server to publish videos on your behalf.
  • We do not sell your data. Tokens and logs stay on your server.
  • Third parties: Google (Gemini), YouTube, TikTok, and optional services (e.g. Tenor, GIPHY) have their own privacy policies.

If you run the app locally, most data never leaves your machine except for the APIs you choose to use.
